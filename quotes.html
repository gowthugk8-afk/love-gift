III • I • MMXXVI
When we are together, we become like children—laughing, playing, and enjoying the smallest moments. In those times, it feels like it’s just us, caring only about each other and not about the rest of the world. That freedom to be ourselves is what makes our bond so special. And when life asks us to be serious, we stand strong together. But when we want to laugh, play, and feel that childlike joy again, we do it together—and that is the beauty of our love.
